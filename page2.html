Mitochondria

The Mitochondria, often referred to as the powerhouses of the cell, can be likened to a power plant within our cellular cityscape. Just as a power plant generates energy to sustain various city components and systems, the Mitochondria produce adenosine triphosphate (ATP), the cellular currency of energy. This energy is essential for powering diverse cellular functions, making the Mitochondria the lifeblood of our cell city.
